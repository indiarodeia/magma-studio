Simon R live set on Facebook

Published on 12-04-2020

Simon R recorded a Live Set for facebook with all originals sounds. This 30 minutes ride will take you to a good place. Inspired what he has in the Studio, he made this musics using Korg Minilog, Elecktron Digitone, Arturia MiniBrute 2S, Arturia DrumBrute, Rolland TR-8, Korg Volca Bass, Pioneer RMX1000, Machine and Ableton Live.
